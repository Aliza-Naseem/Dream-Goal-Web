Creek Dhow Cruise

Step aboard a traditional wooden dhow and cruise along the historic Dubai Creek, where the city’s rich heritage meets charming waterfront views. Enjoy a peaceful evening surrounded by the lights of Old Dubai and its iconic skyline.

Our Creek Dhow Cruise includes a delicious international buffet dinner, soft music, and live entertainment. It’s the perfect way to unwind with family, friends, or loved ones while soaking in the cultural beauty of Dubai.

Book Your Dubai Creek Dhow Cruise Today

Get in touch with us to check available dates, package details, or group bookings. Our team is ready to help you plan a relaxing evening on Dubai’s historic waters.
