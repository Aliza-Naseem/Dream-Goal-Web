About Us

At Dream Goal Tourism and Travel, our mission is to make every journey smooth, memorable, and stress-free. Based in Abu Dhabi with three convenient branches, we offer comprehensive services including visa assistance for over 15 countries, Umrah packages by bus and air, flight ticket bookings, and custom holiday plans tailored to your needs.

With a team of experienced travel consultants, we pride ourselves on transparent processes, personalized support, and a high success rate in visa approvals. Whether you’re planning a spiritual trip, a family holiday, or a business visit, we’re here to guide you every step of the way — turning your travel dreams into reality.
