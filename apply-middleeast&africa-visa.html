Required documents for getting Middle East & Africa visa:

  • Passport: Original and copy, valid for at least 6 months.

  • Emirates ID: A copy of your Emirates ID.

  • No Objection Letter: From the company or sponsor of the applicant.

  • Bank Statement: copy of the last 6 months' Statement Stamp from the Bank.

  • Return Ticket: Copy of your return flight ticket.

  • Hotel Reservation: Copy of your hotel booking at your destination.

  • Passport-Sized Photos: Two recent photos with a white background.

  • Residence Visa: Residence visas should have a minimum validity of 6 months.

  • Cover Letter: Optional but recommended to explain the purpose of your visit.

  • Trade licenses copy If your company owners or partners.
