Musandam Dhow Cruise

Escape to Nature with a Scenic Musandam Dhow Cruise in Dibba.

Musandam Dhow Cruise Dibba

Embark on a breathtaking journey along the Musandam coastline with our traditional Dhow Cruise in Dibba. Surrounded by majestic mountains and crystal-clear waters, this full-day experience offers a perfect escape from the city.

Enjoy a relaxing cruise with onboard refreshments, a buffet lunch, snorkeling, and swimming opportunities. Whether you're seeking adventure or serenity, the Musandam Dhow Cruise promises unforgettable memories for families, friends, and couples alike.

Book Your Musandam Cruise Adventure Today

Get in touch with us to check availability, pricing, and group options for the Musandam Dhow Cruise. Our team is ready to help you plan a relaxing and unforgettable day on the water.

Terms & Conditions

Resident visa holders require prior border permission to enter Dibba Musandam. You need to send us the passport copy along with the visa page latest by 3 working days prior to travel to enable us to do the needful for Border Pass

Guest with Tourist Visa must send Passport copies minimum of 24 hours before the tour

Both Resident & Tourist Visa holders must carry their original Passports to enter Dibba Musandam

The above tour is on sharing basis

Guest may carry Swim Wear and fishing equipment
