2-Star Umrah Package by Air

Experience a budget-friendly Umrah journey with our 2-star air package, including visa assistance, return flights, basic hotel accommodation near Haram, and transport arrangements — all designed for a smooth and affordable pilgrimage.

    Package Overview

  • Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

  • Itinerary: 3 Nights in Makkah, 2 Nights in Madinah

  • Departure: Every Monday via Saudi Airlines from Dubai Terminal 1

  • Transportation: Haramain High-Speed Train from Madinah to Makkah

  • Accommodation: Standard hotels within walking distance to the Haram in both cities

Travel & Accommodation Details

Flight

Direct flight from Dubai to Madinah with 23kg checked baggage and 7kg hand carry allowance.

Madinah Hotel

Jayden Hotel or equivalent, located approximately 300 meters from Masjid al-Nabawi.

Makkah Hotel

Infinity Hotel or equivalent, situated around 550 meters from Masjid al-Haram.

Note

Hotel assignments are subject to availability but will be of similar standard.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1: Monday – Arrival in Madinah

  • Morning: Depart from Dubai to Madinah via Saudi Airlines.

  • Afternoon: Arrive in Madinah and transfer to the hotel for check-in.

  • Evening: Attend Maghrib and Isha prayers at Masjid al-Nabawi.

  • Optional: Visit Rawdah Sharif (Riyadh al-Jannah) by booking through the Nusuk app.

Day 2: Tuesday – Ziyarat in Madinah

  • Morning: Perform Fajr prayer at Masjid al-Nabawi.

  • 7:00 AM: Embark on a guided tour of Madinah's historical sites.

  • Afternoon: Return to the Haram for Dhuhr prayer.

  • Evening: Free time for personal worship and reflection.

Day 3: Wednesday – Journey to Makkah

  • Morning: Perform Fajr prayer and prepare for departure.

  • 10:00 AM: Visit Masjid al-Shajarah (Miqat) to enter into Ihram.

  • Afternoon: Travel to Makkah via the Haramain High-Speed Train.

  • 2:00 PM: Arrive in Makkah and check into the hotel.

  • Evening: Perform Umrah rituals with guidance from the accompanying guide.

Day 4: Thursday – Ziyarat in Makkah

  • Morning: After Fajr prayer, depart for a guided tour of Makkah's sacred sites.

  • Afternoon: Return to Masjid al-Haram for Dhuhr prayer.

  • Evening: Optional visit to Jannat al-Mu'alla cemetery and additional Tawaf.

Day 5: Friday – Jumu'ah in Makkah

  • Morning: Attend Fajr and Jumu'ah prayers at Masjid al-Haram.

  • Evening: Free time for personal worship and optional additional Umrah with guide assistance.

Day 6: Saturday – Farewell Tawaf and Departure

  • Morning: Perform Fajr prayer followed by Tawaf al-Wida (farewell Tawaf).

  • Late Morning: Return to the hotel for breakfast and check-out.

  • Afternoon: Transfer to Jeddah Airport for the return flight to Dubai.
